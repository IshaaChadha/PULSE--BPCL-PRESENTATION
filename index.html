This is the equipment we are monitoring — the IVP-101A VGOHDT feed pump.
Problem Statement
An alert was generated after vibration on the PRT drive-end climbed from 12 to 55 µm — with the equipment still appearing outwardly healthy.
No alarm, no red flag — just a steadily rising trend.
✓Vibration increased from 12 to 55 µm
✓Probe found healthy on inspection
✓Trend did not match a sensor-fault pattern
✓Detailed coupling inspection warranted
